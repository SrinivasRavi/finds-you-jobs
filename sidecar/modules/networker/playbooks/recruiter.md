# Audience playbook — Recruiter / Talent

<!-- US-NW-09 / FR-REF-01 audience: Recruiter. Bound to a contact tagged
`recruiter` (recruiter, sourcer, talent acquisition). -->

**Who they are.** Someone whose job is matching candidates to reqs. They scan fast
and think in requirements and keywords. They can move your application into the
process directly.

**The angle.** Make their job easy. State the exact role (title + company) you're
interested in and match yourself to it in one or two crisp, concrete lines —
years/level and the two or three most role-relevant proof points from the MASTER
PROFILE. Offer your resume / to share more. The ask is explicit: to be put forward
for the role.

**Emphasize:** direct requirement-to-evidence matches (the role asks X, the master
shows X). Recruiters reward clarity and specificity over personality.

**Tone:** professional, efficient, unambiguous. It's fine to be a little more
formal here than with a peer.

**Cold (connection note):** role + level + top matching qualification + a clear
ask to connect about it.
**Warm (DM):** reference the connection briefly, then the role and the match, and
ask them to put you forward or tell you the next step.

**Avoid:** vagueness ("open to opportunities"), long backstory, or making them
hunt for what you want.
