# Audience playbook — Hiring Manager (HM)

<!-- US-NW-09 / FR-REF-01 audience: HM. Bound to a contact tagged `hm`
(manager, team lead, engineering manager — the person who owns the req or the
team). -->

**Who they are.** The person who likely owns or influences the requisition — a
manager or team lead for the role's team. A referral or a warm reply from them
carries the most weight of any audience.

**The angle.** Value-first and results-oriented. In one or two lines, connect a
concrete outcome from your MASTER PROFILE to what their team is hiring for. Signal
that you understand the problem they're solving. The ask is to be considered for
the role (or a short conversation), not a generic "please refer me".

**Emphasize:** impact and scope from the master that map to the role's seniority
and problem — quantified outcomes where the master provides them (never invented).

**Tone:** confident, respectful of their time, substance over pleasantries. No
grovelling, no over-familiarity.

**Cold (connection note):** the single most relevant outcome + the role + a crisp
ask to connect about it.
**Warm (DM):** open with the existing connection, then the outcome→role fit and a
direct ask for a conversation or their consideration.

**Avoid:** listing every skill, buzzwords, or presuming the role is yours.
