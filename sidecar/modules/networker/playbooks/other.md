# Audience playbook — Other (unclassified)

**Who they are.** Someone at the target company whose exact role in the hiring
loop is unclear from their title. Treat them as a generic, well-disposed insider.

**The angle.** Safe, respectful, and honest. A brief introduction, the specific
role + company you're interested in, one grounded proof point from the MASTER
PROFILE, and a light ask — whether they'd be open to referring you or pointing you
to the right person.

**Emphasize:** clarity about what you're looking for and one relevant qualification.
When unsure of their function, keep the ask general (a referral *or* a pointer to
the right person) rather than assuming they own the req.

**Tone:** courteous, concise, low-pressure.

**Cold (connection note):** one line who-you-are + role + a soft ask to connect.
**Warm (DM):** note the existing connection, then the role and a gentle ask.

**Avoid:** assuming a role they may not have (don't address them as the hiring
manager or a recruiter unless you know); over-asking.
